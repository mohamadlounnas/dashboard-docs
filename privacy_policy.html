Overview: "FiElKhidma" is a centralized service platform where the company creates user accounts to streamline service requests and facilitate efficient communication between users and service providers.

Privacy Policy:

  1. User Accounts Created by the Company:

    • The company creates user accounts, ensuring accurate and verified information. This might involve onboarding users through a secure process that includes identity verification.

  2. Service Categories:

    • The app covers various service categories, and users can request services based on their needs.

  3. Company-Assigned Service Requests:

    • The company receives service requests on behalf of users and assigns these requests to suitable service providers based on their expertise and availability.

  4. Automated Recommendations:

    • The app provides automated recommendations based on the nature of the service request, ensuring users are aware of additional services that might complement their needs.

  5. Real-time Communication:

    • Users can communicate in real-time with service providers through the app. The company may also facilitate communication between users and service providers as needed.

  6. Service Tracking:

    • Users can track the status of their service requests, and the company provides updates on the progress of the assigned service providers.

  7. Payment Handling:

    • The company manages the payment process, ensuring secure transactions between users and service providers. Users might have predefined payment methods on file.

  8. Feedback and Ratings:

    • After each service, users provide feedback and ratings. The company uses this information to assess service provider performance and continually improve service quality.

Privacy and Security Measures:

  • The company ensures the security of user data by implementing robust encryption and verification processes during the account creation phase.
